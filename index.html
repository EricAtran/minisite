GAMEPLAY

There are 2 teams per game, defenders and attackers. Each team has 5 players consisting of 5 different agents.
The attackers job is to plant a spike which explodes after a few minutes, and the defenders are to prevent that from
happening or defuse the spike whenever its planted. The teams can also
win by finishing off the other team but if the spike is planted then that has to be defused anyway
